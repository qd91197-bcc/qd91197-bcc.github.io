About Us

The Late 90s Design Archive is meant to be a website for preserving graphic designs of the past in an easy to use format.

Goals

  • Preserve the iconic works of design created before our time and keep them online
  • Allow other people to upload examples of this kind of stuff for free
  • Introduce more people to this kind of design and hopefully one day we can bring it back.

Target Audience

The target audience for the site is graphic designers looking for moodboards and inspiration for their work.
